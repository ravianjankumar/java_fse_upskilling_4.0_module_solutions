Registration Issues

If you experience problems registering for events, please ensure all form fields are correctly filled out. The email should be valid, and the phone number must be 10 digits.

Events Information

Browse our events by category or use the search bar to quickly find upcoming community activities. Seats for each event are limited and filled on a first-come, first-served basis.

Account Management

Currently, our portal does not require user accounts. All registrations are tracked via email and event selection.

Contact Support

If you need further assistance, please contact us directly via the contact form.
